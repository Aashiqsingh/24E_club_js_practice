There are three types of oprator:
1. unary operator
        - it will required only one operand
        ex : a++ , a-- 
2. binary operator
        - it will required two operand
3. ternary operator
        - it will required three operand


Binary operator :
1. Arithmatic operator : + , - , * , / , % , ** 
2. Assignment operator : = (, += , -= , *= , /= , %=) -- short hand property
3. logical operator : && , || , !
4. bitwise operator : & , | 
5. relational operator/ comparision operator : == , != , <= , >= , === , !==
